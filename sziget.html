Join us for the ultimate music festival experience in 2025! Set on the scenic Óbuda Island in Budapest, our festival offers an unparalleled mix of live performances, art installations, and cultural experiences. With an eclectic lineup spanning rock, pop, electronic, and world music, there's something for every music lover.

Beyond the music, explore vibrant art displays, participate in creative workshops, and immerse yourself in the diverse cultures represented throughout the festival. Camp under the stars, engage in thrilling activities like yoga, paddleboarding, and treasure hunts, or simply wander and soak up the unique atmosphere.

Whether you're here for the music, the art, or the unforgettable vibes, our festival promises an experience like no other in the heart of Budapest.
